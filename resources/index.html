Recognised Certifications

  • Certified Professional in Accessibility Core Competencies (CPACC)

    Recognised globally

  • Web Accessibility Specialist (WAS)

    Recognised globally

  • Certified Professional in Web Accessibility (CPWA)

    Recognised globally, automatically awarded upon passing both CPACC and WAS exams. The highest level of digital accessibility certification.

  • Trusted Tester

    Recognised globally, but holds more weight in North America. Still a great bonus elsewhere.
